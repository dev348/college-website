Diploma in Mechanical Engineering (DME)

Aims of the Course:

The Mechanical Engineering provides a solid foundation for professional engineering practice and further study in Mechanical Engineering or many other related fields. The major requirements develop the students ability to apply scientific principles in the design and analysis of mechanical and energy conversion. Mechanical engineering covers a wide variety of activities from engines, turbines, pumps and compressors, to heat exchangers and pressure vessels, to boilers and steam systems, to hydraulics and fluid flow, to dynamics and static's, to plant and manufacturing operations, to project engineering to project management and several more.


Course duration for FULL TIME:

The Course for the Diploma in Mechanical Engineering shall extend over a period of three academic years, consisting of 6 semesters and the first year is common to all engineering branches.

Course duration for PART TIME :

The Course for the Diploma in Electrical and Electronics Engineering shall extend over a period of four academic years, consisting of 8 semesters and the first year is common to all engineering branches.


Eligibility for FULL TIME:

  • For I year-Passed 10th/SSC/Matric /CBSC/ANGLO
  • For II year (Lateral entry) –Passed 12th science with Vocational/ Technical (or) 10th + (2 years I.T.I) with appropriate specialization.

Eligibility for PART TIME:

  • 10th pass + 2 years work experience (OR)
  • 10th pass + ITI with appropriate specialization.

Vision

 

By imparting technical knowledge to the budding students with knowledge in the basic science and make them full blossomed persons with all round knowledge in physical, mental and intellectual aspect to face any challenges successfully and make great achievements in the growing global range. 

Mission 

To improve the students with knowledge and skills in order to respond to engineering problems in the vast mechanical engineering field. To encourage the students to participate actively in co-curricular and extracurricular activities.

 

To update the students in advanced technologies by conducting guest lectures, seminars and workshops.
